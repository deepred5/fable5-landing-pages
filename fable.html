I

In the beginning, there was a question.

Once, models were measured only in numbers — parameters, benchmarks, tokens per second. Fable was measured in something older: whether it could sit with a hard problem the way a storyteller sits with a long night.

A fable is a small story that carries a large truth. That is the design brief, in five words: carry large truths, lightly.

Fable 5 is Anthropic's most intelligent generally available model — the first of the Claude 5 family, in a new tier that stands above Opus. It reasons longer, holds more, and hallucinates less. But mostly, it listens better.
